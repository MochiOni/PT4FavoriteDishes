The Recipe of My Favorite Dish:

Mango Graham or Crema de Mangga

Ingredients

  • 2pc 210g Graham Crackers
  • 2 Nestle All Purpose Cream
  • Finely Chopped Bits of Mango
  • 210g Crushed Graham Cracker
  • Any Condensed Milk

Directions

  1. Put the All Pupose Cream in A Glass Bowl, Whip the cream until it thickened.
  2. After the cream thickened put some condensed milk and mix.
  3. Now put one layer of the graham crackers in your desire container.
  4. Follow up the whipped cream or the whipped all purpose cream and repeat the same process and sprinkle some mango chopped bits after the cream layer.
  5. Serve the Mango Graham with a sprinkled crushed mango graham and chopped mango on top.
